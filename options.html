Blocked URLs:

The blacklist supports Regular Expression matching.
Make sure to include a ^ (shift + 6) infront of the URL if you don't want to match anywhere in URL.
Entering 
http://www.facebook.com
into the blacklist will block all pages that have that string in the URL.
However, entering 
^http://www.facebook.com
will only match URLs that begin with 
http://www.facebook.com
